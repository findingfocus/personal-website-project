About Me

My name is Paul Thompson, I am an interdisciplinary artist interested in developing software solutions and interactive art. I own a small video production company in Albuquerque, New Mexico, but my recent studies have led to a deepening skill set that now includes Full Stack Web Development and computer science.

C is my favorite programming language, but I use Lua most often when prototyping ideas.

I am analytical, professional, and highly motivated to learn and integrate with a team focused on a digital engineering ecosystem.
