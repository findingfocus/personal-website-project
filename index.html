About Me

My name is Paul Thompson, I am an interdisciplinary artist interested in developing software solutions and interactive art. I own a small video production company in Albuquerque, New Mexico, but my recent studies have led to a deepening skillset that now includes programming and computer science.

I am analytical, professional, and highly motivated to learn and integrate with a team focused on a digital engineering ecosystem.

C is my favorite programming language, but I use Lua most often when prototyping ideas. I built this website with HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I also make simple programs with python.

I will describe that I am seeking to work with a team creating new products. I hope to convey that I am versatile in my programming skillset. Joust

This section will feature three playable game demos that are smaller versions of my bigger game projects already hosted on GitHub.
